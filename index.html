Q & A

For all our friends and family who have lots of questions, please check out our Q & A first!

When is the RSVP deadline?
Please RSVP by May 15th, so we can have an accurate headcount. :)
Can I bring a date?
Please check your invite for your +1!
Are kids welcome?
As much as we love your little ones, we will not be including them in the ceremony or reception. Please reach out if you have questions!
What will the weather be like?
The weather here can be funny. During the day, the weather in Maastricht should be beautiful, averaging 75-80 degrees, but will get chilly at night. Please bring some warm layers for the evening.
Where should I park?
If you're driving in, there is parking available at the venue.
Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?
Our wedding ceremony is outdoors, but our reception will be in a tent :)
Is it okay to take pictures during the wedding?
Yes! We would love for you to take photos and share them! However, please refrain from taking photos during the ceremony.
Will food be served?
Yes, we will be serving a vegan 3 course meal cooked up by Van Oys's renowned 2-Michelin-starred chef Guido Braeken.
