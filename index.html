Travel Options

We're excited to welcome you to our celebration! Here are the best ways to reach our venue:

By Air

The nearest airport is Springfield International Airport (SPI), about 25 minutes from the venue. We recommend arranging a rental car or using a rideshare service like Uber or Lyft.

By Car

Lakeside Gardens is located at 123 Lake View Road, Springfield. Free parking is available on site.

From the north: Take Highway 45 South to Exit 23, then follow Lake View Road for 2 miles.

From the south: Take Interstate 90 North to Exit 115, then follow the signs for Lakeside Gardens.

What's the dress code?

Can I bring a plus-one?

Due to venue limitations, we can only accommodate the guests named on your invitation. Thank you for understanding.

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones, but our wedding will be an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you an opportunity for a night out!

What about gifts?

Your presence is our present! However, if you wish to give something, we've created a registry at WeddingRegistry.com.

Will there be vegetarian/vegan options?

Yes! Please indicate any dietary restrictions in your RSVP so we can accommodate your needs.
